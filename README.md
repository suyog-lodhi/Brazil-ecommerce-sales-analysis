# 🛒 Brazil E-Commerce Marketplace Performance Analysis

**Sales • Categories • Sellers • Payments • Delivery**  
📊 *2025 Power BI Project*

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end business analysis of a Brazilian e-commerce marketplace dataset (2016–2018). The objective was to evaluate overall marketplace performance across:

- Revenue & Sales Trends  
- Product Categories  
- Seller Segmentation  
- Payment Behavior  
- Delivery Efficiency  
- Regional Performance  

The project simulates a real-world business case and translates raw data into actionable insights using SQL and Power BI.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Overview

## 1️⃣ Performance Overview

Provides a high-level snapshot of key KPIs:

- **Total Orders Delivered:** 96,478  
- **Total Payment Value:** $15 Million  
- **Average Order Value (AOV):** $160  
- **Average Rating:** 4.2  
- **Total Items Sold:** 1,10,197 

📌 **Insight:** Revenue growth is primarily driven by higher order volume rather than increased spending per order.

## 2️⃣ Category Analysis

- Top 6 categories contribute **58.7% of total revenue**  
- Bottom 6 categories contribute only **1.03% of revenue**  
- Furniture is a high-volume contributor  
- Watches & Gifts show higher AOV (231)  

📌 **Insight:** Revenue is concentrated in a few dominant categories, while freight-heavy products significantly impact logistics costs.

## 4️⃣ Order Analysis

- Top 3 states (São Paulo, Minas Gerais, Rio de Janeiro) contribute ~66–68% of total orders  
- São Paulo alone contributes **42% of total orders**  

📌 **Insight:** Order activity is heavily concentrated in a few key states.

## 5️⃣ Seller Performance

- **Total Sellers:** 3,095  
- 78% of sellers (0–5K band) contribute only 16.8% of revenue  
- 1.6% of sellers (50K+ band) contribute 32% of revenue  
- 60% of sellers are located in São Paulo  

📌 **Insight:** A small percentage of high-performing sellers drive a disproportionate share of marketplace revenue.

## 6️⃣ Payment Analysis

- 74% of transactions via Credit Card  
- Credit Card Revenue: ~$12M  
- Boleto Revenue: ~$3M  
- Majority of orders paid in single installment  
- Average Payment Sequential: 1  

📌 **Insight:** The marketplace is heavily dependent on credit-based transactions, reflecting installment-driven purchasing behavior.

## 7️⃣ Delivery Performance

- **Average Delivery Days:** 12  
- **Median Delivery Days:** 10  
- **On-Time Delivery Rate:** 97%  
- **Cancellation Rate:** 0.6%  
- **Average Rating:** 4.1  

### Regional Highlights:

- Roraima, Amapá, Amazonas: 25–30 days average delivery  
- Yet maintain ~97% on-time rate  
- São Paulo: 8.7 days average delivery  

📌 **Insight:** Even remote regions maintain strong on-time performance, indicating accurate delivery estimation and reliable logistics.

# 📈 Key Business Insights

- Revenue and orders are regionally concentrated  
- Seller productivity is highly imbalanced  
- Credit cards dominate payment behavior  
- Growth is order-volume driven  
- Delivery operations are stable and reliable  
- Freight-heavy categories increase logistics pressure  

---

# 📌 Business Recommendations

- Improve productivity of low-performing sellers  
- Reduce dependency on core states  
- Promote high AOV categories  
- Maintain strong logistics standards
